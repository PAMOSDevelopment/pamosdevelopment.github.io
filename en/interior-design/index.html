What the project includes

An interior design project involves decisions that shape your everyday life for years to come. We carry it out calmly – typically over about 3–4 months, with the option to speed things up – so that the result is truly refined and cohesive.

  • 3 functional layout variants (2D floor plans) – you choose an arrangement tailored to your habits, with carefully planned space for furniture, kitchen appliances and electronics, plus the option to relocate partition walls.
  • 3 stylistic concepts for each room in realistic 3D visualisations – you'll see your home before it's built.
  • Selection of materials, colours and aesthetic solutions, cohesive throughout the entire interior.
  • Full construction documentation for the contractors: tile layouts, placement of electrical points, joinery drawings and detailed dimensions.
  • A ready-made shopping list of materials and furnishings – you know exactly what to buy and where.
  • 3 rounds of revisions at each stage – we refine the project down to the last detail.
  • A kick-off online meeting, a site survey and a visit to the construction site – we're with you from idea to completion.

About me

Meet the designer

Hi, I'm Paulina

Owner of PAMOS Interior Design

I'm an architect by training, and I've been working in interior design for 9 years. I began my journey at IKEA – it was a truly valuable experience. I also worked as a designer at other companies, gaining experience in both private and public spaces: offices and shops. Eventually I decided to start out on my own.

I specialise in comprehensive interior design and approach each project individually – every interior is unique, just like its owners. Designing is a long process, and behind it lies far more than beautiful photographs. What gives me the greatest satisfaction is seeing the smiles of owners who have made their dream home come true.

Besides designing, I co-run PAMOS HOME – an outlet for furniture and accessories. A few days a week you can find me at the showroom on Krakowiaków, where I'm always happy to help with furnishing your space.
