Our idea

The home we were looking for ourselves

We build in a place where we would want to live ourselves – using the materials and solutions we would choose for our own family. A Panasonic heat pump, ground-floor air conditioning, underfloor heating throughout the house, triple-glazed windows and threshold-free terrace glazing are our standard, not a costly add-on.

Interiors and space

Take a look inside

Living area · 49 m²

A living room full of light

Open to the kitchen and dining area, with a threshold-free opening onto the terrace. Tall, triple-glazed windows let the sun in all day long, and the underfloor heating means you can walk barefoot here even in winter. This is the heart of the home – where the day begins and ends.

Kitchen · 9.9 m²

A kitchen that brings people together

Open to the living room, with an island and space for a family table. Enough room to cook together, and enough calm to gaze out at the garden over your morning coffee.

Upstairs · 4 bedrooms

An upper floor made for rest

The entire upper floor belongs to quiet: four bedrooms, two walk-in wardrobes and a separate utility room to keep everyday life simple. The master bedroom comes with its own dressing room and a view over the greenery.

3 bathrooms

Bathrooms for the whole family (and guests)

Two full bathrooms upstairs and a convenient WC on the ground floor. The plumbing and screeds are prepared for your choice of tiles and fittings – or for a ready-made design from PAMOS Interior Design.

Form and surroundings

A terrace, garden, space and a setting you'll fall in love with

A modern form with a graphite metal roof, on a fully fenced 600 m² plot. Threshold-free HS sliding glazing opens the living room onto the terrace and garden, an automatic gate welcomes you from day one, and an extra gate leads out towards the forest.
